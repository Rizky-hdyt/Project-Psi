# Dokumen Pendukung 3
# Future Product Roadmap
**Produk:** Freelance City Index: Yogyakarta Edition · **Versi:** Final

Roadmap mengunci batas MVP dan memberi rumah bagi seluruh fitur out-of-scope agar tidak menyelinap ke V1.

---

## V1 -- MVP (semester ini)

**User side**
- Landing Page
- Persona Quiz (**4 persona**: Tech Professional / Creative Professional / Student & Fresh Graduate / Digital Nomad; input budget, prioritas internet, **prioritas community**, environment)
- Recommendation Engine (weighted scoring 4 indikator x 5 wilayah)
- Algorithm Explanation (Step 2)
- Result Page (ranking + skor 4 indikator + UMK/coworking/internet)
- Why This Match (blok penjelasan)
- District Detail Page (+ District Snapshot)

**Admin side**
- Admin Login
- Dashboard (ringkasan + monitoring timestamp + peringatan)
- Data Management (form input/validasi)
- Audit Log

**Non-fungsional:** web mobile-responsive, Bahasa Indonesia, hosting & DB tier gratis.

---

## V2 -- Personalisasi & Perbandingan
> Memerlukan prasyarat **akun pengguna** (login/registrasi).

- **Login / Registrasi Pengguna** *(enabler)*
- **Save Result** -- menyimpan hasil lintas sesi
- **History & Favorites**
- **Compare District** -- bandingkan wilayah berdampingan
- **District Visualization** -- grafik skor (radar/bar) per wilayah
- **Interactive Map / Google Maps API**
- **Email Notification** saat data diperbarui

---

## V3 -- Ekosistem & AI

- **AI Assisted Recommendation** -- rekomendasi dengan asistensi AI/natural-language
- **Accommodation Recommendation** -- rekomendasi akomodasi/kost terintegrasi
- **Coworking Recommendation** -- rekomendasi coworking spesifik
- **Community Ecosystem** -- forum, review/rating, chat, integrasi media sosial
- **Generative AI** -- ringkasan/penjelasan generatif
- **Multi-language Support** & **Mobile App Native (iOS/Android)**
- **Push Notification** & **Social Media Integration**
